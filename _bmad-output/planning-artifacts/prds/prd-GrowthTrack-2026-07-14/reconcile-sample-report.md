# Reconciliation — sample-whatsapp-report.md vs PRD

Scope: PRD FR-6 (Scheduled Report Generation & Send) and Dashboard FR-3, checked against `_bmad-output/specs/spec-growthtrack/sample-whatsapp-report.md`.

## Covered

- FR-6's Consequences explicitly enumerate the sample report's core fields: "YTD/MTD sales, Achievement %, Growth %, team performance, top/focus brand, doctor list" — this is a near-complete field-for-field match against the sample (YTD Sales, MTD Sales, MTD Achievement, MTD Growth, Team A/B/C, Top Brand, Focus Brand, Top Doctors).
- Glossary §3 anchors "Daily Report" directly to the sample file: "content and layout match `sample-whatsapp-report.md`" — a clean, unambiguous traceability link.
- UJ-2 (Farhan's morning-report journey, §2.3) accurately mirrors the sample's field list — "YTD/MTD sales, his Achievement % and Growth %, his team's standing, this month's top and focus brands, and a prioritized doctor list" — without erroneously adding fields not in the sample.
- FR-4 (Brand Performance Lists) explicitly states Top/Focus Brand data is "computed once, consumed by both the Dashboard (FR-3) and the Daily Report (FR-6) so the two never disagree" — good single-source-of-truth framing for the Top Brand/Focus Brand fields shared by both views.
- FR-5 (Prioritized Doctor List) explicitly ties its output to "the relevant recipient's Daily Report (FR-6)," accounting for the sample's "Top Doctors" section.
- Team performance by Sales Team appears in both FR-3 (dashboard) and FR-6 (report), consistent with the sample's Team A/Team B/Team C breakdown.
- Achievement %/Growth % formula ambiguity is explicitly flagged rather than silently guessed — tagged `[ASSUMPTION]` in §3/§14 and listed as Open Question 2 (§13).
- The PRD is generally Bangladesh-locale-aware elsewhere (FR-6's default 07:00 Asia/Dhaka assumption; §10 Bangladesh PDPA discussion; addendum §A2's BDT-billing local resellers), even though this awareness isn't connected to currency display (see Gaps).

## Gaps

- **Vision (§1) contradicts the sample report and FR-6 itself on "Today's Sales."** §1 states: "The daily report itself surfaces not just raw numbers (today's/MTD/YTD sales, Achievement %, Growth %)..." — but the sample report has no "Today's Sales" line (only YTD Sales and MTD Sales), and FR-6's own Consequences list only "YTD/MTD sales" with no "today's" figure. This isn't merely an unexplained omission — it's a direct internal contradiction between the Vision narrative and the FR-6/sample source of truth, and should be corrected (delete "today's" from §1, since Today's Sales is Dashboard-only per FR-3).
- **Dashboard-only fields (Today's Sales, notification status) are not flagged as dashboard-exclusive, and §4.2's own description asserts the opposite.** FR-3 requires seven fields including "Today's Sales" and "notification status," neither of which appears in the sample WhatsApp report. §4.2's Description says the Dashboard shows "the numbers the Daily Report also carries, but always current rather than as of the last scheduled run" — language implying the *same* field set at different freshness, not a *superset*. Nothing in §4.2/FR-3/FR-6 explicitly states the Dashboard's field set is a strict superset of the Report's (Today's Sales and notification status are dashboard-exclusive by design: intraday sales isn't meaningful in a once-daily report, and a report can't sensibly carry its own delivery status). As written, an unexplained-inconsistency reading is reasonable rather than a deliberate, documented design choice.
- **Currency/locale formatting ("Cr BDT") is never referenced as a requirement anywhere in the PRD.** The sample report displays sales in "Cr BDT" (crore Bangladeshi Taka) and its own footnote flags this as locale-defining. Yet FR-3, FR-6, the Cross-Cutting NFRs (§8), and the Assumptions Index (§14) never state that Sales/YTD/MTD figures must render in crore units with BDT currency labeling — despite the PRD assuming a Bangladesh deployment elsewhere (Asia/Dhaka default schedule, Bangladesh PDPA in §10, BDT-billing resellers in addendum §A2). This is a real display/formatting requirement (crore vs. lakh vs. raw BDT vs. USD, symbol vs. code) left completely unaddressed — it should be either an explicit FR-3/FR-6 consequence or an added Assumption.
- **FR-4/FR-5's richer field sets are silently reduced in the sample report without the PRD noting the reduction.** FR-4 specifies brand list entries include "Brand Name, Sales, and Growth," but the sample shows only a bare brand name for Top Brand and Focus Brand (no figures). FR-5 specifies doctor entries include "Doctor Name, Territory, and Target Priority," but the sample's "Top Doctors" list shows names only. FR-6 doesn't clarify whether the WhatsApp report is intentionally a name-only rendering of richer FR-4/FR-5 data (plausible, given WhatsApp message brevity) or whether this is an unaddressed gap between what's computed and what's sent.
